Society for Applied Sciences and Technologies

The Society for Applied Sciences and Technologies (SAST) is a dynamic organization dedicated to fostering collaboration, research, and development in the fields of applied sciences and technologies. We bring together professionals, scholars, industrialists, NGO’s and enthusiasts from these disciplines to explore innovative solutions, advance education, and promote the integration of science and technology in today's rapidly evolving world.

•• Our Purpose

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To be a leading organization that empowers individuals and institutions to achieve excellence in applied science and technology, while fostering global collaboration and knowledge-sharing for societal advancement.

Our Mission

  • To promote the advancement and application of scientific and technological knowledge for societal benefit.
  • To encourage research, innovation, and interdisciplinary collaboration.
  • To provide a platform for knowledge exchange among scientists, technologists, and students.
  • To organize seminars, workshops, and training programs for capacity building.
  • To foster sustainable solutions addressing environmental, social, and economic challenges.
  • To recognize and honour outstanding contributions in science and technology.

•• Who We Are

Bridging Research & Real-World Solutions

The Society for Applied Sciences and Technologies (SAST) is a professional organization committed to advancing the practical application of science and technology for the betterment of society. We serve as a collaborative platform for scientists, researchers, academicians, and innovators to share knowledge, address global challenges, and drive technological progress.

Research & Innovation

Advancing interdisciplinary research and fostering innovative applications of science and technology.

Education & Training

Enhancing knowledge and skills through workshops, seminars, and professional development programs.

Conferences & Events

Organizing national and international forums to share ideas, research outcomes, and best practices.

Networking & Community

Building a collaborative platform for scientists, technologists, educators, and entrepreneurs.

Sustainability & Social Impact

Promoting the responsible use of science and technology to support environmental conservation and societal well-being.

Membership

Whether you are a scientist, researcher, or student, SAST welcomes you.

Exclusive Member Benefits

Event Discounts & Recognition: Special discounts on SAST events and recognition for contributions.

Global Networking: Connect with experts, educators, and researchers worldwide.

Learning Opportunities: Workshops, webinars, and conferences for professional skills.

Awards & Honors: Eligibility for prestigious awards presented by SAST.

Honorary Membership

Prestigious recognition for individuals who have made exceptional contributions to applied sciences. Honorary members are invited to participate in key activities.

Fellow Membership

Conferred upon distinguished professionals for outstanding academic achievements, leadership, and impactful service to the society. Gain prominent visibility and influence.

Corporate Membership

For organizations committed to advancing applied sciences. Benefit from exclusive networking, collaborations, and specialized events.

Apply for Membership

To apply, submit your detailed CV to president.sast@gmail.com

Cancellation Policy: Members may cancel their membership at any time. However, no refunds will be issued for the current membership period. To cancel, please contact us at president.sast@gmail.com.
